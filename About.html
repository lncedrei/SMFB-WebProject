SAN MIGUEL CORPORATION

The commitment of San Miguel Corporation to deliver quality goods in every Filipino home, beloved brands that make life better, has become a reality. San Miguel has the widest and most diverse product line in Philippine history, covering the food, beverage, and packaging industries. When it comes to San Miguel Corporation, only superlatives will do, as it is the leading beer distributor in the world.

San Miguel is the biggest and oldest brewer in Southeast Asia. It is also the biggest and most profitable company in the Philippines. San Miguel has a 90% share of the Filipino beer market, with its flagship beer dominating the market. However, it was not beer alone that put San Miguel on top of the Southeast Asia brewer category. Other products of the firm include agricultural feeds, fresh and processed meats, dairy products, coconut products, hard liquor, non-alcoholic drinks, and packaging materials such as glass containers, corrugated cartons, aluminum cans, and metal crowns/caps.

Don Enrique Ma Barretto de Ycaza, the founder, built the brewery, the first in Southeast Asia, in 1890 under the name La Fabrica de Cerveza de San Miguel. The founder named his company after the district in Manila in which he resided. By 1913, the company was exporting its beer, which carried the company's name, to Hong Kong, Shanghai, and Guam.

SAN MIGUEL FOOD AND BEVERAGES

San Miguel Food and Beverage, Inc., being the leading food and beverage company in the Philippines, was established as early as 1890 as the first brewery in Southeast Asia. The company has a 90% local market share, with its beer under the company name being its most popular brand at present. The company is recognized as Southeast Asia’s largest and oldest brewery. The conglomerate has produced the most diversified product offerings in Philippine history with its roster of popular brands, making life a celebration through its commitment to delivering the best quality products to each and every Filipino home.

Apart from this, the company has a large market share in different segments of the market, such as hard liquor, powdered juices, and carbonated drinks. The company's renowned brands, such as Magnolia, have traditionally led the vast majority of the frozen dessert segment of the market, while its large food business encompasses fresh and processed meats and poultry, dairy products, as well as agricultural feeds. The San Miguel Food and Beverage segment of this company has sustained its position in the regional marketplace through its competitive practices as well as its long-range planning to serve the needs of the nation in the food, beverage, and packaging industries. The company is a profitable manufacturer as well as a significant contributor to the national economy.
