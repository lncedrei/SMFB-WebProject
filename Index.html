SAN MIGUEL FOOD AND BEVERAGES

One of the oldest, biggest, and most varied corporations in the Philippines. San Miguel Food and Beverage (San Miguel) is involved in a variety of industries, including food and beverage, packaging, real estate, oil refining, energy, and infrastructure. Additionally, it includes the following business groups: San Miguel Foods, Magnolia, and The Purefoods-Hormel Company for food operations, and San Miguel Brewery and Ginebra San Miguel for alcoholic beverages.
